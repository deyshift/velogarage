Features

Everything in your garage,
nothing you don't need.

Built for cyclists who actually wrench their own bikes. No subscriptions, no bloat — just your components and their mileage.

🔗
Strava sync
Connects to your Strava account and automatically calculates mileage per bike from your ride history. No manual entry.
⛓️
Lube-aware chain tracking
Different intervals for wax (400 km), dry lube (175 km), wet lube (400 km), and ceramic (650 km). Switch lube types any time.
🚨
Health status at a glance
Green, amber, and red health bars for every component. Know what needs attention before you head out.
🏠
Multi-bike garage
All your Strava bikes in one place. Track separate component sets per bike — road, gravel, MTB.
📋
Maintenance log
Record every service — replacement, lube, clean, adjust — with date and odometer. Full history per component.
📴
Works offline
All data lives on your device in encrypted local storage. Sync when you have signal, ride any time.
How it works

Set up in minutes.

Connect once, then let VeloGarage do the maths on every ride.

1
Connect your Strava account
Tap "Connect with Strava" and authorise read-only access to your activities. VeloGarage never posts or modifies anything on your behalf.
2
Your bikes appear automatically
Every bike registered in Strava shows up in your garage with its total mileage. Primary bike is highlighted.
3
Add your components
Tap a bike and add components — chain (pick your lube type), cassette, tires, brake pads, rotors. VeloGarage records the current odometer as the install point.
4
Go ride
After each ride, open VeloGarage and pull to sync. Mileage updates across all components on that bike. Health bars turn amber at 80%, red at 100%.
5
Log service, reset the clock
After wrenching, tap "Log Service" on the component. The mileage counter resets. Full service history is always one tap away.

Privacy

Your data stays yours.

VeloGarage is built to be trustworthy. No accounts to create, no data sold, no servers storing your maintenance history.

🔒
Encrypted on-device storage
All maintenance data lives in an encrypted SQLite database on your phone. It never leaves your device.
🔑
Secure token storage
Strava tokens are stored in iOS Keychain / Android Keystore — hardware-backed encryption.
👁️
Read-only Strava access
VeloGarage only requests activity:read_all scope. It cannot post, modify, or delete anything on Strava.
📂
Open source
The entire codebase is on GitHub. Inspect it, fork it, self-host the backend. No black boxes.
